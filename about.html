About Us

What we offer

We provide cutting-edge technology and services to manufacture all types of precision components.

Sun Tech Precision was established for Aerospace parts manufacturing, on October 2017. We work hard with objective of Quality and in time delivery, providing machining solution in the field of Aerospace.

Our plant is facilitated with,

  • Turning Machine centre
  • Vertical Machining Centre with 4th Axis
  • Turn Mill Machine with Y axis
  • CMM (Co-ordinate Measuring Machine)
  • VMM (Video Measuring Machine)
  • Mitutoyo Height Master
  • Mitutoyo Surface Roughness Tester
  • With Precision Quality Hand Instruments
